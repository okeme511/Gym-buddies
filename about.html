ABOUT GYM BUDDIES


The gym buddy community started in 2008 in a small town called Carluke. At that time, young people were inspired by the guys who were working out hard and getting big. David Chapman, who is only 26 years old, came up with the idea of "together we can grow." He started the Gym Buddy platform and shared information about it with the youths. The idea went viral, and a lot of youths were interested in it. Now, Gym Buddies is well-known all over the UK. Interested gym members can download the app and enter their postcode to see thousands of other gym members in their area and make plans to meet up.
